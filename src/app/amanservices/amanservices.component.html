Description

AMAN aims to integrate all existing Fire Alarm systems at all types of properties in Sharjah with the Civil Defense Control Room, Which will improve the monitoring and speed of response to incidents in real time which is essential to save lives. AMAN delivers the alarm signal without human intervention and with high accuracy using the existing communication infrastructure at an affordable cost.

Objectives

AMAN is a first of its kind joint venture between Sharjah Asset Management Holding (SAM), the investment arm of the Government of Sharjah, and Apleona HSG (formerly Bilfinger HSG International Facilty Management), a global facilities management service provider delivering world class services in the UAE.
